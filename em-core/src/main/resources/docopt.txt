Usage:
  em [ -h | -v | -vv ]
  em <script> ...
  em --activity=<activity> ...
  em --activity-types
  em --activity-help <activitytype>

Options:
  -h                               Show help.
  -v                               Show version, short form.
  -vv                              Show version, long form, with artifact coordinates.
  --activity-types                 Show the available activity types.
  --activity-help <activitytype>   Show help for an activity type.
  --activity=<activityDef>         Specify an activity to run.
